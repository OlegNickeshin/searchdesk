FAQ

Products are selected using text matching (title, brand, category). If a rule matches the query, it is applied first. Pinned products are always shown on top, then the rest are sorted by score.

A rule defines how search results should be boosted for a specific query. Rules can use exact or contains matching and apply different boosts.

Boosts increase the score of products that match certain attributes (brand, category, gender). Format example: Nike:50,Adidas:20.

When enabled, products that are in stock receive a large score boost and appear higher in the results.

Pinning forces a product to appear at the top of the ranking for a specific rule. Lower position number means higher placement.
